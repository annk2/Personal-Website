About

Anna Kawasaki is a junior at Portland State University where she is majoring in Computer Science. She currently has special interests in web development, graphics, and natural language processing. Currently, she is working as a Technical Course Support Specialist, helping students working in the lower-division programs. Though she is still exploring different career paths, she hopes to ultimately focus on making technology more accessible rather than more advanced. When she is not busy hitting her head against her keyboard trying to figure out a bug that takes 2 seconds to fix, she enjoys creating digital art and reading books about history.
